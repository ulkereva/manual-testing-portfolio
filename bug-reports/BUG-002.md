| **Priority** | Low |
| **Status** | Open |

---

## Summary
After removing an item using the "Remove" button on the Products listing page, the cart badge counter does not update until the page is refreshed.

---

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Log in as `standardemail@gmail.com` / `secret_sauce`
2. On the Products page, click **Add to cart** on any product
3. Confirm the cart badge shows **1**
4. Click the **Remove** button on the same product
5. Observe the cart badge in the top-right corner

---

## Expected Result
Cart badge disappears immediately (or updates to 0) right after clicking **Remove**.

---

## Actual Result
Cart badge still shows **1** after clicking Remove. Badge only disappears after manually refreshing the page (F5).

---

## Impact
- Users may think the item was not removed
- Could lead to duplicate remove attempts
- Breaks expected real-time UI feedback

---

## Notes
- Reproducible 100% of the time in Chrome 124
- Not tested in Firefox/Safari yet
- This is a UI synchronization issue — likely a missing state update in the frontend
